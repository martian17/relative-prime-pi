calculatig π using random number GCD

Brief description

The probability that two random integers are relatively prime is 6/π²
Therefore, from here, we can reverse the equation and calculate pi to be
π=√(6/prob)
Where prob is the ratio of relatively prime numbers.

Idea from: https://link.springer.com/content/pdf/10.1007/3-540-51084-2_23.pdf
